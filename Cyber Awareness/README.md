## 2026 Cyber Awareness Challenge

- Open the 2026 Cyber Awareness Challenge to any point.
- Open your developer tools. This is F12 in most browsers.
- Open the Console tab of your developer tools.
- Enter: `location.href="https://cs.signal.army.mil/usermngmt/CyberAwareness_2026/pages/disacac18_02_army.html"` into the console.
- Run the command.
- Click the Verify Training button.
- Complete.

*These instructions work for 2024/2025* Cyber Awareness Challenge as well.
